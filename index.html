About Kash

Built by Necessity

Kash began with a simple question: Why is tracking money so complicated?

As a 21-year-old student in Dhaka, I found myself frustrated by the lack of clean, efficient tools to manage my finances. Everything felt cluttered or bloated. The turning point came when I watched my girlfriend struggle to track her expenses in a standard notes app, it was manual, disconnected, and clearly not designed for the way we live today.

I realized there wasn't a tool that offered the simplicity I wanted, so I decided to build one. With zero prior coding experience, a lot of curiosity, and the vast resources of the internet, I designed Kash.

What is Kash?

A personal finance manager built for the modern, fast-paced life in Bangladesh, bridging your Bank, bKash, Nagad, and cash in one place.

Kash isn't just a project, it's a solution born from my own need for clarity and a testament to what you can build when you decide to stop waiting for someone else to solve the problem.
